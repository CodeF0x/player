Violin

A minimalistic and simple music player for desktop systems.

  • Features

Dark Theme

The app is dark themed by default. (Light theme may follow).

Fast and simple

No confusing UI elements and no loading time at all enable you to really enjoy your music.

Cross-platform support

It doesn't matter on what system you are on, Violin supports Windows, Linux (Debian based), and macOS.
